I'm Patrick - a web developer and musician. For work, I build library tools for the University of Pennsylvania. I'm particularly interesting in devloper tooling and DX. 

I make music as well - my most recent project is made entirely from vinyl samples. I spend too much money on samplers and records.

## Now
- I'm building [milkcrate](https://milkcrate.fm) - a discovery layer for discogs sellers. Trying to make digging for records online more fun than spreadsheet-surfing.
- I just finished The Radio Show (coming soon) - a sample odyssey about life, death, sex, love, resurrection, grief, and so much more. I love talking about sampling - ethics, legality, and spirituality.
- I'm trying to get into running. I talked to my addiction specialist about what addiction and substances do for me. He told me I sound like all the runners in his life, so here we are.
